📘 OEE Explanation (Overall Equipment Effectiveness)

OEE = Availability × Performance × Quality

▶
Availability (Uptime)

Ratio of actual machine runtime vs. planned production time. Affected by breakdowns, scheduled maintenance, and product changeovers.

▶
Performance (Throughput)

Ratio of actual production speed vs. design speed. Affected by minor stoppages, reduced speed, and idling.

▶
Quality (Yield)

Ratio of good-quality products vs. total production. Affected by manufacturing defects and scrap.

Industry Benchmark (World-Class OEE): 🟢 ≥ 85% Excellent 🟡 60-85% Average 🔴 < 60% Needs Improvement
