1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In modern office environments, group food & beverage ordering is a daily operational 
routine. However, manual coordination by group hosts leads to severe productivity 
loss, cash flow bottlenecks, and frequent financial deficits due to forgotten 
transfers or shipping fee miscalculations.

OrderFlow is a specialized Enterprise Fintech solution engineered to automate the 
entire group order lifecycle from item aggregation, cost sharing, dynamic QR payment 
generation, to real-time status tracking and vendor dispatch.

2. BUSINESS OBJECTIVES & KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Coordination Time Reduction: Reduce average host coordination time from 50 minutes 
  to under 5 minutes per order (-90%).
- Deficit Elimination: Reduce payment loss/deficit rate from 12% to 0.00% by enforcing 
  upfront dynamic QR payment verification.
- Process Efficiency Gain: Boost process efficiency by +80% compared to manual messaging 
  threads (Slack/Zalo).
- Member Satisfaction: Achieve >95% positive feedback on group payment experience.

3. AS-IS VS TO-BE PROCESS GAP ANALYSIS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[AS-IS PROCESS - MANUAL (HIGH RISK)]
1. Host posts text query in group chat ("Who wants drinks?").
2. Team members send individual text requests across unorganized threads.
3. Host manually compiles item count on paper/Excel.
4. Host places order with vendor and pays 100% out-of-pocket (Cash Flow Risk).
5. Host manually calculates individual costs + shipping fee splits (45 min Time Sink).
6. Host posts bank details and sends individual reminder messages.
7. Team members transfer money later (often forgotten or miscalculated -> 12% Deficit).

[TO-BE PROCESS - AUTOMATED (ZERO RISK)]
1. Host initializes Order Session in OrderFlow; countdown timer starts (e.g., 15 mins).
2. Team members open self-service link and select items into shared draft.
3. System automatically calculates individual splits, taxes, and shipping discounts instantly.
4. System generates dynamic VietQR/Momo QR codes tied to exact member amounts.
5. Members scan and pay directly; system receives webhook confirmation in real-time.
6. Once 100% funds are verified, system automatically dispatches order to vendor.

4. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS MATRIX (FRD)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FR-01: Order Session Management
- Description: System shall allow Host to initiate, pause, extend, or close an order 
  session with an active countdown timer.
- Priority: High (Must Have)
- Acceptance Criteria: Given a host starts a session, when timer reaches 00:00, order 
  draft automatically locks for new item entries.

FR-02: Self-Service Item Aggregation
- Description: System shall render a real-time collaborative item list where participants 
  can add, edit, or remove their selected items.
- Priority: High (Must Have)
- Acceptance Criteria: Item changes shall update grand total and per-person split 
  dynamically without page refresh.

FR-03: Dynamic Cost & Fee Split Engine
- Description: System shall calculate individual shares including base item price, 
  proportional VAT, and equal/proportional shipping fee allocation.
- Priority: High (Must Have)
- Acceptance Criteria: Sum of individual splits MUST equal 100% of vendor grand total.

FR-04: Dynamic QR Payment Integration
- Description: System shall generate dynamic VietQR / Momo / VNPay QR codes containing 
  exact amount and payment reference code (e.g., "OF-1223-HN").
- Priority: High (Must Have)
- Acceptance Criteria: Scanning QR auto-populates exact amount and recipient account.

FR-05: Real-Time Payment Webhook & Status Tracking
- Description: System shall monitor payment status per participant (Paid / Unpaid) 
  and display progress visually.
- Priority: High (Must Have)
- Acceptance Criteria: When payment webhook triggers, status updates to "Paid" within <1s.

FR-06: Automated Slack/Teams Reminder Trigger
- Description: System shall provide a 1-click or automated reminder action to notify 
  unpaid participants via chat bot.
- Priority: Medium (Should Have)
- Acceptance Criteria: Clicking "Remind" sends targeted notification with payment link.

FR-07: Executive Reporting & Export
- Description: System shall generate exportable PDF/CSV reports detailing session 
  history, total spend, time saved, and vendor breakdown.
- Priority: Medium (Should Have)

5. NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS (NFR)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Performance: Page load time < 1.2s; QR generation time < 300ms.
- Reliability: 99.9% uptime for payment status webhooks.
- Security: TLS 1.3 encryption; zero storage of sensitive banking credentials.
- Usability: Responsive Mobile-First design adhering to WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility.
